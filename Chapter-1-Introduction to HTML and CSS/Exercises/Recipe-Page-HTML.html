Quick and Easy Tiramisu

Enjoy your delightful tiramisu pudding.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar
  • 28 soft ladyfingers
  • 2-1/2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1/2 cup boiling water
  • 1 tablespoon baking cocoa

Instructions

  1. Whisk milk and pudding mix for 2 minutes
  2. Beat whipping cream until soft peaks form
  3. Add pudding to whipped cream and beat until incorporated
  4. Dissolve coffee in boiling water
  5. Layer ladyfingers, coffee mixture and pudding in dish
  6. Sprinkle top with cocoa powder

What make tiramisu pudding the best pudding?

Here's why Tiramisu pudding is one of the best puddings:

Tiramisu pudding is made with espresso-soaked ladyfingers, creamy mascarpone custard, cocoa powder, and, on occasion, a splash of rum or brandy.
The layers result in a nuanced, sumptuous flavour character.

The crispy ladyfingers add a lovely textural contrast to the smooth and creamy Mascarpone custard.
This keeps each mouthful exciting.

Tiramisu's sweet and bitter flavours come from ladyfingers, chocolate, coffee, and creamy custard.
The combination of sweetness and bitterness is particularly desirable.

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian dessert made with high-quality ingredients such as mascarpone cheese, espresso, and cocoa.
The Italian influence elevates it.

Tiramisu is best made with freshly whipped mascarpone and espresso-dipped ladyfingers. This helps the flavours explode.

Tiramisu's elegant design, stacked in a glass and coated with cocoa powder, matches its delicious taste.

This dish is versatile and can be served individually or in large trays for groups. Both simple and complicated versions exist.

What all goes with tiramisu pudding

Here are some products that go nicely with tiramisu pudding.

Espresso or strong coffee:
Tiramisu is commonly made using espresso-soaked ladyfingers; therefore, mixing it with espresso is a classic.
The bittersweet coffee complements the sweet custard.

Fresh fruit:
Berries such as raspberries, blueberries, or strawberries provide a refreshing, sharp contrast to the creamy tiramisu.
Their sweetness and acidity offset the richness.

Whipped cream:
A dollop of freshly whipped cream on top adds a burst of airy sweetness.

Chocolate shavings:
sprinkle chocolate shavings or cocoa nibs on top to provide extra chocolate flavour.
Dark chocolate works well.

Cookies:
Butter cookies, shortbread cookies, or vanilla wafers serve as a crispy textural contrast.

Nuts:
Chopped roasted nuts such as almonds, hazelnuts, and pistachios can add crunch. Their toasted taste provides depth.
